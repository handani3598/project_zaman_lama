Activity 1 : Gotong-Royong (GROUP 5)

For our usrah section, we planned to organize gotong-royong to contribute ourselves in IIUM community. We were divided into five groups and each group had to suggest the location for them to conduct the activity. My group decided that we would clean the Musolla of Mahallah Nusaibah but due to certain circumference, we changed our location to Study Lounge of Mahallah Sumaiyyah

On 6th December, my group consisting of Aina, Ainaa and Najwa started gather at Mahallah Summayyah's cafe before proceeding to the study lounge. Arriving at the location, we split our task and I was assigned to clean off the book rack and swiped the dust on it. Later, me and Najwa swept off the carpet and make sure the carpet was cleaned. It would be much better if we had the vaccuum with us, but I think our effort may bring benefits to the user of this lounge later. It did not take us too much time to clean and it was definitely a good time to show our cooperation eventhough we occasionally talked during the class hour. They were all very kind and cooperate in making sure our activity went successfully.

It also came to my understanding how important it is to be clean for ourselves and the surroundings. Abu Malik Al-Ash`ari (may Allah be pleased with him) reported that the Messenger of Allah (peace and blessings be upon him) said:

"Purity is half of iman (faith)."
