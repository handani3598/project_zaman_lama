With news about UPU 2018/19 slowly streaming in, students are nervously anticipating applying to UPU 2018/19, hoping to secure a place at universities such as Universiti Malaya, Universiti Putra Malaysia, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia, Universiti Sains Malaysia, Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia or the 15 other remaining institutions. It can all get a little overhwelming, as UPU 2018/19 is no small matter. With that in mind, here is your guide to UPU 2018/19.

UPU for the 2018/19 academic session will open on February 5, 2018 at 12.00pm in the afternoon and then close on April 10, 2018 at 12.00am. You need to buy a unique BSN pin number to apply for UPU 2018/19. You can start to buy it from February 5, 2018 until April 9, 2018. If you want to buy your BSN pin number individually, you can do so at BSN counters. If the applicant/applicant's parents posses a BSN Giro Account, then the purchase can be made via ATM

  • Students will only have to fill in their name, family details. extra co-curriculum activities & chosen programme/s at university.
  • Students will not need to fill in their SPM results.
  • SPM results will automatically be transferred into the system based on data sourced from the Education Department.
  • Programmes that appear will correspond to respective student's results.
  • Programmes that student do not qualify for will not appear as an option.
  • Prevents students from applying for programmes they do not qualify for.
